How to Take the Typing Test

Welcome to TypeRacer! Here's how to take the typing test:

  • Start the Test: Begin typing in the input area to start the test automatically.
  • Change Difficulty: Use the dropdown menu to select a difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard). The sample text will update accordingly.
  • Complete the Test: Press the Enter key to finish the test and see your results.
  • Retry the Test: Click the "Retry" button to reset the test and try again at the same difficulty level.
  • Real-Time Feedback: As you type, correctly typed words are highlighted in blue, and incorrectly typed words are highlighted in red.

Good luck, and enjoy improving your typing speed!
